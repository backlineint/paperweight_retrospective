Paperweight was...

a digital humor magazine founded in 2012 by Chris Duffy and Brian Perry. The app was supported by over 200 Kickstarter backers and was featured on Wired.com. Beautifully designed and interactive, Paperweight featured writing, illustrations, multimedia pieces, and games from up-and-coming humor writers around the country. Paperweight interviews included Pulitzer Prize-winning humorist Dave Barry, standup comedians like Hari Kondabolu and Jonah Ray, and Saturday Night Live writer Zach Kanin.
